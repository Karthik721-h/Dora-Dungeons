* Buy Weapons

(name, description, price)

Speak:
"Here are the available weapons:"

"Ashbringer, a legendary blade, costs 10 gold..."
(continue for all)

Then:
"say the name of the weapon you wished to buy"

UI:

* Show 6 buttons (name + price)

On purchase:
IF enough gold:

* Deduct gold
* Add weapon to player.weapons

Speak:
"Purchase successful. The weapon has been added to your arsenal."

IF insufficient gold:
Speak:
"You do not have enough gold for this purchase. Continue exploring to earn more."

4. SELL ITEMS

UI:

* Show inventory items as buttons (with sell price)

VOICE:
Speak:
"These are the items in your inventory:"

Example:
"You have a healing potion worth 15 gold, and two elixirs worth 10 gold each."

Then:
"Say the name of the item you wish to sell."

On sell:

* Remove item
* Add gold

Speak:
"Item sold successfully. Your gold balance has been updated."

Speak:
"Your inventory is empty. Explore the dungeon to collect items."

5. UPGRADE ARMOR

Armor rules:

* Level range: 1 → 3
* Default: Level 1

Upgrade cost per level:

* Level 1 → 2
* Level 2 → 3

UI:

* Show each armor + upgrade cost

VOICE:
Speak:
"These are your available armors for upgrade:"

Example:
"Judgment Armor can be upgraded to level 2 for 20 gold."

Then:
"say the name of the weapon you wish to upgrade"

On upgrade:
IF enough gold:

* Deduct gold
* Increase level

Speak:
"Upgrade successful. Your armor is now stronger, and your gold has been adjusted."

IF insufficient gold:
Speak:
"You do not have enough gold to upgrade this armor."

IF no armor:
Speak:
"You currently have no armor to upgrade. Continue exploring to find gear."
