You are a senior game systems engineer.

Implement a Level Scaling System in Dora Dungeons that makes each dungeon level progressively harder and more engaging.

---

## GOAL

Each new level should:

* Increase enemy difficulty
* Improve boss strength
* Increase rewards (XP, Gold)
* Keep gameplay balanced and fair

---

## STRICT RULES

* DO NOT modify API routes
* DO NOT break existing combat system
* EXTEND logic inside GameEngine only
* MUST be deterministic per level

---

## STEP 1 — CREATE SCALING CONFIG

Create a new module:

LevelScaling.ts

---

Define:

getLevelMultiplier(level: number)

Example:

return 1 + (level - 1) * 0.2

---

Meaning:
Level 1 → 1.0x
Level 2 → 1.2x
Level 3 → 1.4x
Level 5 → 1.8x

---

## STEP 2 — SCALE ENEMY STATS

When enemies are generated:

Apply multiplier:

enemy.hp *= multiplier
enemy.attack *= multiplier
enemy.defense *= multiplier

---

Round values to integers

---

Ensure:

* No stat becomes 0 or negative

---

## STEP 3 — SCALE BOSS SEPARATELY

Boss multiplier:

bossMultiplier = multiplier * 1.5

---

Apply to:

* HP
* Attack
* Defense

---

Boss should feel significantly harder than normal enemies

---

## STEP 4 — SCALE REWARDS

Increase rewards based on level:

XP:

xp = baseXp * multiplier

Gold:

gold = baseGold * multiplier

---

Ensure:

* Rewards feel meaningful
* Not overpowered

---

## STEP 5 — SCALE STATUS EFFECT INTENSITY (OPTIONAL SAFE)

Example:

* Burn damage increases slightly
* Poison duration increases slightly

---

DO NOT create new effects
ONLY scale existing ones

---

## STEP 6 — SCALE DUNGEON VARIETY

As level increases:

* Increase chance of multi-enemy encounters
* Increase trap difficulty
* Increase rare room frequency

---

Example:

if (level > 3):
allow 2–3 enemies per encounter

---

## STEP 7 — BALANCE CAP (IMPORTANT)

Add soft cap:

IF multiplier > 3.0:
multiplier = 3.0

---

Prevents game from becoming impossible

---

## STEP 8 — ENSURE DETERMINISM

Scaling must depend ONLY on:

* dungeonLevel
* existing seed

---

NO randomness outside seed

---

## STEP 9 — ADD LEVEL CONTEXT TO NARRATION

Enhance narration:

Examples:

Level 1:
"A quiet dungeon… something feels weak."

Level 3:
"The air grows heavier. Enemies feel stronger."

Level 5+:
"A deadly aura fills the dungeon. Only the strong survive."

---

Inject this into dungeon start narration

---

## STEP 10 — VALIDATION

Test:

Level 1:

* Easy enemies
* Low rewards

Level 3:

* Noticeably harder enemies
* Better rewards

Level 5:

* Strong enemies
* Boss is challenging

---

Verify:

* No stat overflow
* Combat still works
* No crashes

---

## EXPECTED RESULT

* Smooth difficulty curve
* Better progression feeling
* Increased player engagement

---

## STOP AFTER THIS STEP

Do NOT implement payment yet
Do NOT change frontend
