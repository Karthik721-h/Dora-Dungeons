* Trigger armor-based damage reduction logic

---

* If empty → no reduction applied

---

## STEP 3 — DAMAGE REDUCTION RULES

IF enemy.isBoss = false:

* Level 1 → 25% reduction
* Level 2 → 50% reduction
* Level 3 → 75% reduction

IF enemy.isBoss = true:

* Level 1 → 10% reduction
* Level 2 → 30% reduction
* Level 3 → 50% reduction

---

## STEP 4 — APPLY REDUCTION

Formula:

reducedDamage = incomingDamage * (1 - reductionPercent)

* Round final damage (Math.floor or Math.round)
* Apply reduced damage to player HP

---

## STEP 5 — RETURN COMBAT RESULT

Include in combat result:

* original damage
* reduced damage
* armor used (name + level)

---

## STEP 6 — NARRATION HOOK (IMPORTANT)

Return structured data ONLY.

Example:
{
action: "defend",
armorUsed: "Judgment Armor",
level: 2,
damageReduced: 15
}

DO NOT generate narration text here (handled in Phase 3.5 system)

---

## STEP 7 — EDGE CASES

* If no armor → full damage applied
